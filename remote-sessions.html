The Process
How It Works
01
Book Your Session
Fill out the form below with your project details, preferred dates, and what you need. We'll confirm and send you a Muse session link.
02
Download Muse
Download the free Muse app at musesessions.co. It works with any DAW — Pro Tools, Ableton, Logic, whatever you use.
03
Join the Session
Click your session link at the scheduled time. Share your instruments, DAW audio, and microphone in real time — we'll hear everything like we're in the same room.
04
Walk Away with Files
We share final files directly through Muse's built-in file transfer. Stems, mixes, masters — whatever the session calls for, delivered clean.

Muse in the Studio

The virtual studio is real now.

Muse is not a video call with a shared screen. It's a full audio routing platform built specifically for music production — the difference in session quality is night and day compared to Zoom or FaceTime.

We use Muse for everything from songwriter sessions and vocal tracking to mix reviews and artist development check-ins. If you've got a DAW and an internet connection, we can work together.

Come Prepared
What You Need

You'll Need

  • A computer running Mac (10.15+) or Windows 10+
  • The free Muse app installed — download at musesessions.co
  • A DAW of your choice (or just a microphone — no DAW required for some sessions)
  • A stable internet connection — wired is best but WiFi works
  • Headphones or studio monitors to avoid feedback during the session

Bring to the Session

  • Reference tracks — songs that capture the vibe you're going for
  • Any existing session files or stems you want us to work with
  • A clear idea of your goals for the session — the more specific the better
  • Your instruments tuned and ready — don't spend session time on setup
  • An open mind — some of the best work happens when you let the session breathe
